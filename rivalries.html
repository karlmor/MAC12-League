History

A rivalry born from their shared love of the Cincinnati Bengals. Every draft becomes a strategic battle for Bengals players, with both managers trying to secure the likes of Ja'Marr Chase and Joe Burrow. Their matchups are always high-scoring affairs, with the winner earning year-long bragging rights and first dibs on Bengals players in the next draft.

The Stakes

The loser must change their team name to praise the winner's favorite Bengals player for the following week. Additionally, the winner gets priority on Bengals waiver wire pickups for the next matchup.
